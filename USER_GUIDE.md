# Tradkuserp – Quick User Guide

## How to Make a Receipt

**Receipts are created automatically when you record a payment on an invoice.**

1. Go to **Invoices** and open the relevant invoice
2. Click **Record Payment**
3. Enter the amount, payment method (Cash, Bank Transfer, Card, Mobile Money, Cheque), and reference
4. Click **Save Payment**
5. The receipt is created and appears under **Receipts**

You can view all receipts at **Receipts** in the sidebar.

---

## Select Job

**Job** links a quotation or invoice to a project. Use it to track which projects your quotes and invoices relate to. It’s optional.

---

## Products & Services (Items)

- **Item name** – The product or service name (e.g. "Solar panel installation"). This is what appears when you search in quotations/invoices.
- **Description** – Extra details for that line (e.g. "Gateway Stream ADs API Development"). Used per line on quotes/invoices.
- **Category** – Optional grouping (e.g. Solar, Wiring).

---

## Create New → Quotation

Opens your **Quotations** list (drafts and sent). Click **New Quotation** there to create a new one.

---

## Send via WhatsApp

Currently sends a **text summary** (invoice number, total, due date, balance). It does **not** attach the PDF. You can download the PDF separately and send it manually, or the customer can request it.

---

## Currency

Quotations and invoices support three currencies: **USD** ($), **ZIG** (Z$), and **ZAR** (R – South African Rand). Select the currency when creating a quotation or invoice.

---

## Edit Customers

Click the **edit (pencil)** icon next to a customer on the Customers page to update their details.
